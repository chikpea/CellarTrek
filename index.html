Questions

Frequently asked

Is CellarTrek really free?
Yes. The free plan gives you full cellar management, WSET Level 1, wine groups and events, and venue connect — with no time limit. The free plan is supported by wine brand advertising. Premium removes all ads and unlocks unlimited AI and the full WSET curriculum for .25/month.
What AI model powers CellarTrek?
CellarTrek uses Claude by Anthropic — the same AI that powers much of the enterprise AI world. Claude is known for its accuracy on factual subjects like wine, and its ability to write beautifully and reason carefully.
How accurate is label scanning?
Label scanning uses Claude's vision capability to identify the producer, vintage, region, and grapes from a photo. It works well on most printed labels. If a label is handwritten, heavily worn, or very unusual, we recommend using the manual search fallback.
Is the WSET content official?
CellarTrek's education module follows the WSET Level 1, 2, and 3 curriculum structure and covers the same topics, but it is not an officially approved WSET programme. To receive a WSET qualification, you need to enrol with a WSET Approved Programme Provider and sit their exam. CellarTrek is the best study companion for that journey.
Does it work outside the US?
CellarTrek was built for the world from day one. The app is available in 9 languages: English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Mandarin, Japanese, Tagalog, and Korean. It works in any country with an internet connection.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel from within the app settings at any time. You keep Premium access until the end of your billing period, then revert to the free plan. Your cellar data is never deleted.
I run a wine bar. Is there a plan for venues?
Yes — CellarTrek for Venues is built for wine bars, restaurants, wineries, and hotels. The Starter plan is free and includes member onboarding via QR code, a venue feed, events, and marketing materials. Venue Pro (99/month) unlocks unlimited members, the shop marketplace, and analytics. Visit our venue page to learn more.
